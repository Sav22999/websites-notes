Privacy Policy acceptance

From the 4.0 version of Notefox, you are able to create a Notefox Account to sync your data across devices. This is a great feature but, because it's external to your web browser service, it requires a small extra step to ensure your privacy.
You can also use Notefox without creating an account.
In this case, your data will be stored in your browser's local storage only, and it won't be collected anything.
To continue to use Notefox, you need to accept the Privacy Policy, which will be applied only if you create an account.
This doesn't mean that you have to create an account mandatorily.
You have already accepted the Privacy Policy of Notefox.
(Only if you create a Notefox account)

We follow three simple principles

Only necessary data is collected

We only collect the data that is necessary to provide the service – such as notes, settings, IP address, ….

End-to-end encryption

We use end-to-end encryption to protect your data, so only you can access it.

Nothing is shared

We don't share your data with third parties.

You can delete a Notefox Account in anytime.
Notefox Account uses end-to-end cryptography, so if you lose your password, you will lose all your data.
If you delete your account, all your data will be deleted permanently and cannot be recovered.
You could be interested also in the Terms of Service and the Privacy Policy of Notefox.
