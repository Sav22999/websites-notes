Consent Experience

Notefox is a free, open-source add-on that lets you take and sync notes securely. You are in control of what data is sent to our servers.

Telemetry Data ▼

What they are:

Anonymous usage data sent to Notefox, including account status, anonymous user ID, browser language, actions (save/delete note), context, website URL (if applicable), browser and OS info, and Notefox version.

How they are used:

Used only for anonymous internal statistics, feature usage analysis, and product improvement. Data are never shared with third parties or used for marketing.

See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for details.

Error Logs Data ▼

What they are:

Anonymous technical error data including error messages, context (page or popup), date/time, website URL (if applicable), Notefox version, and anonymous user ID.

How they are used:

Used exclusively to detect and fix bugs, improve add-on stability, and enhance user experience. Data are never shared for marketing or statistics.

See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for details.
